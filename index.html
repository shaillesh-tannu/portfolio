About

Payments is where I started.
Financial infrastructure is what I’ve spent a decade mastering.

18+ years in engineering. 10+ across payments, fintech, and crypto. I lead teams that own mission-critical financial infrastructure — the kind where a single miscalculated entry triggers a regulatory inquiry, not just a bug ticket.

At Coinbase, I owned the ledger modernisation roadmap — automated accounting across 350+ fund flows, compressed settlement from T+30 to T+1, and built the tax engineering function from 3 to 15 engineers in a single quarter to deliver IRS digital asset reporting for 70M+ US users. I also pioneered AI-first engineering in a regulated environment — 80% of PRs authored via Claude with compliance guardrails in production.

At Nium, I grew an engineering org from 10 to 40+, delivered cross-border payment infrastructure from zero across 100+ currencies and 600+ bank accounts, and built in-house reconciliation that replaced a $250K/yr vendor — all at 99.999% uptime.

Before that, I founded Trice Technologies — an eLearning product that reached 100K+ licences and exited at 10x.

“I hire for judgment, coach for growth, and build environments where engineers ship with confidence in regulated environments. Every system I’ve built was designed to be audit-ready by default — not retrofitted for compliance.”
